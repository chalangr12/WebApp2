Services

Data Destruction

We understand that your data is your data. That means that the data will never be anyone else's. And that is our guarantee. That your data will be wiped before anything is shredded. The way this is accomplished is through proprietry software strictly designed to ensure randomize hard drives with 0's and 1's. 8 passes are done on the hard drive to ensure that the data is no longer accessable.

E-Waste Recycling

At Petabyte Recyclers we do not look at your old electronics as trash or garbage, we look it as a learning tool that can be passed on like a book. That's why at Petabyte we donate 10% of the computer that we deem we refurbish and donate to local schools, churches, mosques, orphanages, etc. Petabyte is dedicated to the community that gives it business, and we feel that we should give it back also where we can.

Degaussing

We understand that there is some data that cannot be sold and refurbished for all data of the sensitive nature there is a two step process Degaussing and shredding. Degaussing, commonly called erasure, leaves the domains in random patterns with no preference to orientation, thereby rendering previous data unrecoverable. Once that is done, the hard drives face shredding and shipping to a third party to be melt down.
